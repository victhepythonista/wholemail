## How to create an app password for your Gmail account ?

1. Go to your Google Account.
2. Click on the Security tab.
3. Under Signing in to Google, click on 2-Step Verification.
4. Click on App passwords.
5. Enter a name for your app password. This will help you remember which app you created the password for.
6. Click on Generate.
7. The app password will be generated and displayed on the screen.
8. Copy the app password and save it in a safe place. You will need it to authenticate your Python script when you send an email.
